# -OLIST-STORE-PERFORMANCE-ANALYSIS
**Project Overview**
This project focuses on analyzing the performance of the Olist e-commerce platform using real-world transactional data. The objective is to uncover insights related to customer behavior, payment patterns, delivery performance, and customer satisfaction through data analytics and visualization.

**Objectives**

  -Analyze order trends based on weekdays and weekends.
  -Identify high-rated (5-star) orders and preferred payment methods.
  -Evaluate delivery performance across product categories.
  -Compare product prices and payment values to understand additional costs.
  -Measure the impact of delivery time on customer satisfaction.

**Tools & Technologies**

  -SQL (MySQL) – Data extraction, KPI calculation, and analysis.
  -Excel / Power BI / – Data cleaning, visualization, and dashboard creation.
  -PowerPoint – Presentation of insights and business recommendations.

**Dashboard & Visualizations**

  Interactive dashboards were created to visualize KPIs, trends, and performance metrics, enabling clear and actionable business insights.

**Key Insights**

  -Faster delivery times strongly correlate with higher customer review scores.
  -Credit cards are the most common payment method for high-rated orders.
  -Weekend order volume shows distinct customer purchasing behavior.
  -Delivery delays negatively impact customer satisfaction.

**Conclusion**

  The analysis highlights operational efficiency, especially reducing delivery time, as the most critical factor for improving customer satisfaction and overall business performance. Data-driven insights from this project can support strategic decisions in logistics, customer experience, and sales optimization.
